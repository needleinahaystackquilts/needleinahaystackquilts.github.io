  1. Press your completed quilt top and remove any loose threads from both the front and back of the quilt.
  2. Trim backings square so that they can be pinned onto the frames correctly.
  3. Cut quilt backs and batting to be three to four inches longer than the quilt top on all sides.
  4. Please do not baste or pin quilt top, batting, and backing together.

  • You may provide your own batting or purchase batting from me.
